Ans 4. HTML tags are keywords that have a unique meaning to the browser. They start with < and ends with >. 

       HTML elements are contained inside the tags and they carry the content.

       Ex: <p>Lorem ipsum</p> , here <p> is the opening tag and </p> is the closing tag. Lorem ipsum is the element in the tag.




Ans 5. <!DOCTYPE> is information to the browser about what document type to expect. They are not case sensitive and do not require a closing tag.
